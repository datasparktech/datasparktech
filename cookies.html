Cookie Policy

What cookies and similar technologies datasparktech.com currently uses — and doesn't.

Effective date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last updated: January 1, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website you visit. They are widely used to make websites function, function more efficiently, and to provide reporting information to site owners. Similar technologies include local storage and tracking pixels.

2. Cookies We Currently Use

As of the effective date above, datasparktech.com does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. We have not integrated tools such as Google Analytics or advertising pixels. The Site's contact and application forms submit directly to our form-processing provider (Web3Forms) without setting cookies on your device.

We reserve the right to introduce analytics, functional, or advertising cookies in the future as the Site evolves. If we do, we will update this Cookie Policy accordingly, and the update will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

3. Third-Party Services

The Site loads web fonts from Google Fonts via Google's content delivery network, and form submissions are processed by Web3Forms. These third-party services may, independent of any action by us, process limited technical data (such as IP address) as part of delivering their service, in accordance with their own privacy practices:

  • Google Fonts: policies.google.com/privacy
  • Web3Forms: web3forms.com/privacy

4. Managing Cookies in Your Browser

Even though this Site does not currently set tracking cookies, your browser may still allow you to view, manage, and delete cookies from any site you visit, including limiting or blocking cookies entirely. Refer to your browser's help documentation for instructions:

  • Chrome: support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647
  • Firefox: support.mozilla.org/kb/firefox-privacy
  • Safari: support.apple.com/guide/safari
  • Edge: support.microsoft.com/microsoft-edge

Blocking cookies generally will not affect your ability to browse this Site, since we do not currently rely on cookies for core functionality.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time, particularly if we adopt new analytics or tracking technologies. Material changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page periodically.

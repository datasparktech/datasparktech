PRICING

Three ways to work with us.

Custom software doesn't price like a subscription. Here's how engagements are structured, what's included either way, and how to compare the three models side by side.

PROJECT-BASED

Fixed-scope project

A defined project with a clear start and finish — a new app, an integration, a migration.

  • Fixed price, agreed before work starts
  • Detailed scope & timeline document
  • Milestone-based delivery and check-ins
  • Best for a single, well-defined deliverable
Request a quote
ONGOING

Monthly retainer

Ongoing development, maintenance, or support at a predictable monthly rate.

  • A set number of hours or a dedicated capacity block each month
  • Covers maintenance, small features, and support together
  • Unused hours can typically roll into backlog work
  • Best for products that need continuous iteration
Discuss a retainer
STAFF AUGMENTATION

Dedicated team

One or more DataSpark engineers embedded with your team, full- or part-time.

  • Priced per engineer, per month
  • Works inside your existing tools, sprints, and standups
  • Scale the team up or down as the project changes
  • Best for longer-term or larger-scale builds
Talk about capacity

03

Work begins

Accessibility Statement

Our commitment to making datasparktech.com usable for everyone.

Last updated: January 1, 2026

Our Commitment

DataSpark Tech LLC is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people of all abilities. We are actively working to improve the accessibility and usability of datasparktech.com for everyone, including people who rely on assistive technologies such as screen readers, keyboard navigation, or voice control software.

Standards We Aim For

We aim to meet or exceed the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, published by the World Wide Web Consortium (W3C). These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible for people with a wide range of disabilities.

What We've Built In

  • Semantic HTML structure to support screen readers and assistive navigation
  • Keyboard-accessible navigation, including the mobile menu and FAQ accordion
  • Text alternatives for meaningful visual elements
  • Color combinations chosen with contrast in mind
  • Responsive layout that supports browser zoom and varying screen sizes

Ongoing Efforts

Accessibility is an ongoing effort, not a one-time fix. As we add new pages and features to the Site, we review them with accessibility in mind. We welcome the opportunity to hear from users about specific barriers they encounter, since real feedback is often the fastest way to find and fix issues that automated tools miss.

Known Limitations

Despite our best efforts, some parts of the Site may not yet be fully accessible. This is a new and evolving website, and we are continuing to test and improve it. If you encounter any barrier while using this Site, please let us know using the contact information below so we can address it.

Third-Party Content

Where the Site links to or embeds third-party content or services (such as form processing or job application links you provide), we cannot guarantee the accessibility of those third-party experiences, though we choose our vendors with accessibility and usability in mind where possible.

Feedback

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of datasparktech.com. If you encounter an accessibility barrier, please contact us with the URL of the page, a description of the issue, and the assistive technology you were using, if applicable:

DataSpark Tech LLC
Dallas, TX
Email: connect@datasparktech.com

We aim to respond to accessibility feedback within a reasonable timeframe and to address reported issues as promptly as practicable.

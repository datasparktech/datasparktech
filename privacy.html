1. Introduction

DataSpark Tech LLC ("DataSpark Tech," "we," "us," or "our") provides custom software, IT, and artificial intelligence services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit datasparktech.com (the "Site"), submit a form, apply for a position, or otherwise engage with us. By accessing or using the Site, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this policy, please do not access or use the Site.

We may offer our services to individuals and businesses located both within and outside the United States. This policy is designed to give a clear, comprehensive account of our practices regardless of where you are located, but it is governed by United States and Texas law as described in Section 15.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

2.1 Information you provide directly

  • Contact and inquiry information: name, email address, phone number, company name, and the details you submit through our "Start a project" form, including service of interest, estimated budget, and timeline.
  • Application information: if you apply for a position through our Careers/Apply page, we collect your name, email, phone number, LinkedIn profile URL, the role you're applying for, a link to your resume, and any additional information you choose to share.
  • Correspondence: any information you provide when you email us directly or otherwise communicate with us.

2.2 Information collected automatically

Like most websites, our hosting and infrastructure providers may automatically log limited technical information such as IP address, browser type, device type, referring pages, and general usage patterns, for security and operational purposes. See our Cookie Policy for details on cookies and similar technologies.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to inquiries and provide quotes, proposals, and information about our services;
  • Evaluate and process job applications;
  • Communicate with you about your project, engagement, or application;
  • Operate, maintain, secure, and improve the Site and our services;
  • Enforce our Terms of Service and other agreements;
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations; and
  • Protect the rights, property, and safety of DataSpark Tech LLC, our clients, and others.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Where applicable law requires a legal basis for processing personal data, we rely on one or more of the following: (a) performance of a contract or steps taken at your request prior to entering into a contract; (b) our legitimate business interests in operating, marketing, and protecting our business, provided those interests are not overridden by your rights; (c) your consent, where you have given it; and (d) compliance with a legal obligation.

5. How We Share Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers. We use third-party vendors to operate the Site and process form submissions, currently including Web3Forms (form-to-email delivery) and Google Fonts (typography delivery via CDN). These providers process data only as necessary to provide their service to us.
  • Legal compliance. We may disclose information where required to comply with applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental request, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of DataSpark Tech LLC, our personnel, clients, or others.
  • Business transfers. If DataSpark Tech LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of business assets, information may be transferred as part of that transaction, subject to standard confidentiality arrangements.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, including maintaining business records, responding to inquiries, evaluating repeat or delayed engagements, complying with legal obligations, resolving disputes, and enforcing our agreements. Where no specific retention period is legally mandated, we determine retention periods based on the nature of the information, the purpose for which it was collected, and our legitimate business needs. We reserve sole discretion to determine appropriate retention periods consistent with this policy.

DataSpark Tech LLC is based in the United States, and our team and infrastructure providers may be located in the United States, India, and other jurisdictions. If you are located outside the United States, please be aware that information you provide to us may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States and other countries whose data protection laws may differ from, and may be less protective than, those of your home jurisdiction. By using the Site or submitting information to us, you consent to this transfer, storage, and processing.

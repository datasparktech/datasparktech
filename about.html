OUR STORY

Built to work like an in-house team

DataSpark Tech LLC is an IT services and custom software firm co-founded by Keyur Shah and Varsha Shah, based in Dallas, Texas. We work with small and mid-sized businesses that have outgrown spreadsheets and off-the-shelf tools, but don't need — or want — a large agency.

Every engagement is scoped and delivered directly by the people writing the code, so nothing gets lost in translation between sales and delivery. Our team spans the US and India, which means longer overlap with your business day and a wider bench of engineering talent than a single-location shop — and we build AI into our own delivery process, not just into what we ship.
